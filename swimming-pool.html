Indoor pool

The indoor swimming pool has a length of 25 meters and a depth of 1.80 to 2.00 meters, divided into several swimming corridors. The water in the pool is purified with special German bio preparations and the temperature is maintained at 27 °C, making it the perfect choice for aqua activities both in summer and winter.

Unlike most pools available at the other fitness clubs, which are mainly made for relaxation and light workouts, our swimming pool at TOP G Fitness is designed to enhance the skills of both underwater and sporting events.
